Biography

Hi, I am a PhD student of Legal Tech in the Department of Informatics at the Technical University of Munich. Before transferring my PhD to TUM, I was a PhD student at L3S Research Center. Previous to that, I worked as a speech scientist at Nuance Communications/Cerence. I did my master in Cultural and Cognitive Linguistics at LMU Munich. My research interest is NLP in LegalTech, Computational Social Science and Computational Linguistics.
